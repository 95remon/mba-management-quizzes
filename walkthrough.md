## How to Use
1.  **Open** the HTML file in your browser (Chrome, Edge, etc.).
2.  **Interactive Mode**: Click on options to see if you are correct. The correct answer and justification will appear.
3.  **Study Mode**: Use the buttons at the top to "Show All Answers" for reviewing.
4.  **Print to PDF**:
    *   Click the green **"Print to PDF"** button at the top of the page.
    *   Or press `Ctrl + P`.
    *   Select "Save as PDF" as the printer.
    *   *Note*: The print layout is optimized to hide the buttons and show clearly formatted question cards with answers visible (if you want an answer key version, click "Show All Answers" before printing, or rely on the print style which forces answers to show).
