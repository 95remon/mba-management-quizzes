Question 1 Strategy
A SWOT analysis combines __________.
  • (a) Internal Strengths/Weaknesses and External Opportunities/Threats
  • (b) Internal Opportunities and External Strengths
  • (c) Financial goals and HR metrics
  • (d) Past and Future plans
Correct Answer: (a)
SWOT matches Internal capabilities with External conditions.
Question 2 Planning
A plan that outlines the alternative courses of action if things go wrong is a __________.
  • (a) Strategic Plan
  • (b) Contingency Plan
  • (c) Budget
  • (d) Procedure
Correct Answer: (b)
Contingency planning deals with 'What If' scenarios.
